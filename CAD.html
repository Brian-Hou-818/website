Personal Project

Custom FPV Drone


Project: Engineered a custom 5-inch FPV drone using Solidworks and Betaflight
Design: Developed a lightweight carbon fiber frame in Solidworks to optimize the center of gravity and ensure structural durability for high-speed maneuvers.
Hardware: Designed custom 3D-printed TPU mounts for the FPV camera and antenna, providing vibration isolation and impact protection for sensitive electronics
Versatility: Streamlined the frame geometry to lower the center of gravity, resulting in improved flight stability and more predictable handling during high-velocity maneuvers



Personal Project

Custom Macro Keypad


Project: Designed a 6-key keypad using Solidworks, Arduino Pro Micro, and a custom PCB
Design: Utilized Solidworks for the structural design of the keypad, ensuring ergonomic and efficient use
Hardware: Integrated an Arduino Pro Micro into the custom PCB for seamless functionality
Customization: The keypad is fully reprogrammable using the Arduino IDE, allowing users to assign any desired key or command
Versatility: The flexibility of programming makes it adaptable to various user requirements, enhancing user experience and productivity
